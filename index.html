Why This Platform Exists

Nepal is entering a decisive energy decade.

Hydropower, transmission infrastructure, electricity trade, and energy economics are shaping the nation's future. This platform simplifies complex technical concepts and makes room for serious policy discussion.

The goal is a long-term knowledge ecosystem around energy sovereignty: practical, technically grounded, and useful to engineers, students, policy makers, investors, and citizens.
